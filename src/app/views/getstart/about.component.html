Rocket Lineup open-source design system

it's a collection of reusable user interface components, guidelines, templates, and other resources

Its main aim is to create modern, intuitive, and engaging experiences for products across all platforms.

The design system groups all the elements that allows you to design and develop a product. It is a set of deliverables that develops constantly with the product and the new technologies. The design system makes your software design and development processes more efficient, our products more consistent, and sound unique.

The goal of Lineup is to improve consistency and quality of the human interfaces. It also helps establish common standards and ease collaboration between different teams.

Lineup is simple, flexible and consistent with subtle interaction patterns spread across. It is comprised of various modules, features such as user customization, dashboards, edit modes, and so on. All the relevant information can be accessed with a single and secured sign in seamlessly. User experience is taken care in an elaborative manner to ensure that the interactions are intuitive and easy.

Follow the best practices when designing and developing the UI, its components and interactions for a rich and user-friendly product experience. These standards help our products put users in control of their data, with all the necessary tools at their fingertips.

Providing Multi themes for better Visualisation

Light Theme

Light theme is the default theme for branding on various Rocket applications. With its light background, dark fonts and vibrant colors, adding the contrast is based on meeting the AA-rating for accessibility.

Dark Theme

A dark theme is a low-light user interface that displays dark surfaces. It reduces the luminance emitted by screens of devices, nonetheless, meeting minimum color contrast ratios. The themes help facilitate use of screen in dark environments, adjust brightness to current lighting conditions, and improve visual ergonomics by reducing eye strain.
