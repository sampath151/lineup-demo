Themes

ASG products can use a light or dark application theme. The theme affects the colors of the application background, text, icons, and common controls. By default, ASG products theme can be decided based on the user's mental module and theme preference.

Light Theme

Light theme is the default theme for branding on various ASG applications. With its light backgrounds, dark fonts and vibrant colors, to add the contrast is on basis of AA rating for accessibility.

Dark Theme

A dark theme is a low-light user interface that displays mainly dark surfaces. It reduces the luminance emitted by screens of devices, nonetheless, meeting minimum color contrast ratios. The themes help facilitate use of screen in dark environments, adjust brightness to current lighting conditions, and improve visual ergonomics by reducing eye strain.

Semantic colors

Semantic colors are used to emphasize an important status or mean something. ASG has five semantic colors that are related to these predefined states

 
Error
#F97E7E

Error colour indicates a bad or negative status or consequence.

 
Warning
#FEDE71

Warning color stands indicates a critical situations of task/action.

 
Success
#42BE65

It indicates a good, positive emotion for a completion of a task.

 
Information
#7789B1

This color is used for an information state.

 
General
#E3E3E3

This color is used for a neutral state.
